# Privacy Policy for RunX

**Last Updated: June 1, 2026**

RunX ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

*   **Account Information:** When you sign up, we collect your name and email address through Supabase Authentication.
*   **Profile Data:** We collect biometrics (age, weight, height) and fitness goals to personalize your training experience.
*   **Location Data:** To track your runs, we collect precise location data (GPS) while the app is in use and, if permitted, in the background.
*   **Fitness Data:** We store run history, including distance, duration, pace, and route maps.
*   **Community Interactions:** Data you share in communities or activity feeds is visible to other users.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

*   To provide and maintain our services.
*   To calculate performance metrics and training plans.
*   To facilitate social interactions and leaderboards.
*   To send notifications related to your training.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud) and local device storage. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Permissions

*   **Location:** Required for GPS tracking.
*   **Notifications:** Required for workout reminders and streak alerts.

## 5. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact support@runx.fit.
